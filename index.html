Welcome to the Color Guessing Game!

The game is simple, you will receive a random hsl color and based on it you'll have to pick 1 out of 6 boxes, one of which holds the correct color while the others contain fakes put there to confuse you!

hsl stands for hue saturation lightness, using these 3 parameters we can make any color out there show up on the screen, it is also my prefer method of creating colors when working!

There also exists a streak counter which will keep track of how many consecutive colors you've gotten right and your high score will go in the top right!

Also if you run into the hsl color and boxes changing when you switch tabs don't worry! That's intended so that it makes it harder for you to cheat! You still have other ways of doing so tho, but you would never do such a thing right? :P

In case you don't know what hsl is don't worry I'll leave a few helpful free resources to get you started on your learning, good luck and have fun!
